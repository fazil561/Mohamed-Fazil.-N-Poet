Mohamed Fazil. N

Poet of "Weight of Soft Things" and "Echoes that Wouldn't Fade"

Fazil is a poet of soft storms and lingering echoes. His words move through love, loss, and the delicate spaces in between—where feelings are not spoken, but felt. Each piece he writes is an attempt to hold onto something fleeting, to give shape to emotions that refuse to fade. His books are not just read—they are experienced, slowly, like a memory returning.
